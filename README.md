# Air Quality Statistical Analysis 

# Project Summary
The analysis showed that urban areas face high pollution levels as compared to non-urban areas.
# Project Objective
To find out wether urban areas experience higher pollution levels than non-urban areas or not.

# Methodology And Technologies
**Descriptive Statistics:** Obtained mean,median, standard deviation and count from the provided dataset.

**One sample t-test:** The test was conducted with 95% confidence level.

**Two sample t-test:** The test was conducted between city (urban) and non-city(rural) areas air quality level.

**Programming language:** Python 3

# Conclusion
### One Sample t-test
>The average log‑AQI is significantly higher than the benchmark (1.5) indicating air quality is on average, worse than that target.
### Two Sample t-test 
>It confirms that urban areas experience significantly higher pollution levels than non‑urban sites according to the analysis.
